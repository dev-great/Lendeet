About Lendeet

Lendeet is a platform for online classified ads containing sections for borrowing and lending of goods, services, volunteer work, gigs, resumes, and chat rooms. It is founded in Nigeria. Depending on the product type and the market's geographic location, classified ads can be either free or paid for.

Mission

To create a platform where Lenders and Borrowers can liaise with ensured security for both parties.

Vision

To create a global environment of possibilities where products everywhere can be for everyone.
