About Lendeet.

Lendeet is a team of young vision-driven people determined to help you save and earn from those things you don’t use too often. We are building Africa’s first blockchain powered Lending marketplace.

Lendeet Mission & Vision.

Our Mission

To create a platform where Lenders and Borrowers can liaise with ensured security for both parties.

Our Vision

To create a global environment of possibilities where products everywhere can be for everyone.

Every Property, An Investment

Have you ever bought something and after a while, you see no need for it anymore? Maybe a lawn mower, and the next time your field would need it is probably two months after? Perhaps it's that car you don't need, taking up space in your garage for weeks now?


During this period, your purchase seems to be nothing more than a liability to you. Lendeet offers an opportunity to turn every property into an investment for you. So, while you currently don’t need your lawn mower, you can make money lending it to people who do! We believe property owners can get more value from their properties; and if they can, why not?

Do More With Less

With the dwindling economy of Nigeria today, and the recurring increase in prices of goods, we think it is time for you to embrace the sharing economy like we have done. Instead of buying that wedding dress, a new camera, a speed boat, camping tents, a projector or every playstation release; you can lend it!

Why Choose Lendeet?

  • Save: Save money on things you only need every now and then

  • Earn: Earn money from things you already have by lending them out

  • Community: Connect with friends and send guarantor requests to friends. The more friends that vouch for you, the more you can lend

  • Security: Lendeet guarantees the security of lends in the duration of the lending process. Learn more

  • Achieve Bigger goals: Do more with less, money isn’t so much of the challenge anymore!

  • Reduce Waste: If you don’t need it, someone else does.

  • Reduce Co2 Emission: We do this by sharing things like cars, generators and other Co2 emitting devices

  • Instant Payment: Cash out your earnings whenever you want
